Product Manager – Technology / SaaS

Role Overview:
We are looking for a dynamic Product Manager to define product vision, strategy, and roadmap for our technology platform. You will be the bridge between business stakeholders, engineering teams, and end-users, leading products from conception to launch and iteration.

Key Responsibilities:
- Define and communicate product vision, strategy, and roadmap aligned with business goals
- Gather and prioritize requirements from customers, stakeholders, and market research
- Write detailed product specifications, user stories, and acceptance criteria
- Partner with engineering, design, data science, and marketing teams
- Monitor product KPIs and drive data-informed decisions
- Manage product backlog and sprint planning in Agile environments
- Conduct competitive analysis and market research
- Lead go-to-market planning and product launches

Required Skills:
- Product Strategy: Roadmapping, prioritization frameworks (RICE, MoSCoW, Kano)
- Agile/Scrum: Sprint planning, backlog grooming, user story writing, retrospectives
- Analytics: Defining KPIs, A/B testing, cohort analysis, funnel analysis
- Tools: JIRA, Confluence, Asana, Notion, Linear, Figma, Miro
- Data: SQL (basic to intermediate), Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude
- Research: User interviews, surveys, usability testing, Jobs-to-be-Done framework
- Documentation: PRDs (Product Requirements Documents), BRDs, specs
- Communication: Executive presentations, stakeholder management

Preferred/Bonus Skills:
- Technical background or experience working with engineering teams
- API product management experience
- Experience with AI/ML product development
- Mobile product management (iOS/Android)
- B2B/SaaS product experience
- Experience with customer journey mapping and persona development

Soft Skills:
- Exceptional communication and influencing skills
- Strong analytical and critical thinking
- Empathy for users and deep customer orientation
- Ability to manage ambiguity and competing priorities
- Leadership without authority

ATS Keywords (High Priority):
Product Management, Roadmap, Agile, Scrum, JIRA, User Stories, A/B Testing, KPIs, Stakeholder Management, PRD, Go-to-Market, Competitive Analysis, SQL, Analytics, Figma, Sprint Planning, Product Strategy, SaaS, B2B, Market Research, Customer Discovery

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Computer Science, or related field (MBA is a plus)
- 3+ years of product management experience in a tech company
- Proven track record of shipping successful products
